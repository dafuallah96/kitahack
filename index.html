2-Day Event!

DSCKita Hack 2020 is a 2-day hackathon presented by Developer Student Clubs (DSC) Malaysia that invites students to develop solutions for local community problems using one or more Google products or platforms.

Participants will be guided through this hackathon by experts from the industry to come up with awesome solutions with great impact and scalability!
